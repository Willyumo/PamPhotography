For over 15 years, photography has been more than a passion — it’s been a journey. What began with portraits, nature, and architectural shots soon led me to work alongside professionals connected with the NFL, The New York Times, and National Geographic. These experiences gave me a level of training and inspiration that no classroom could match. From Maine to Florida, I’ve captured the beauty of landscapes, architecture, and unforgettable events. Along the way, I discovered a true love for boudoir photography — a personal favorite for the creativity, confidence, and emotion it allows me to bring out in my clients. To me, photography is the art of seeing the world differently. My goal is simple: to tell your story, preserve your memories, and create images that reflect both artistry and authenticity. Whether it’s an intimate session or a large-scale event, I bring imagination, professionalism, and passion to every shot.
